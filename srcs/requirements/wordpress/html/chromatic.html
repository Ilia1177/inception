/ CHROMATIC EPHEMERIS

Chromatic Ephemeris is a recurring publication influenced by quantum postulates, manifesting in various forms with variable content. This periodic documentation of color experiments is produced using risography. Through this endeavor, we delve into a quasi-quantum dimension. Join us on this exploration. Welcome to the digital rendition of CHROMATIC_EPHEMERIS.

  • Thought, ideas and emotions are seen like wave :

    Thoughts, ideas, and emotions are perceived as waves: undoubtedly existing, yet in a waveform. They reside within our brains, hearts, and even extend beyond ourselves. Could this webpage be a connection between us?

  • Prints, paper and ink can be perceived as particles :

    Prints, paper, and ink can be regarded as particles: occupying space, existing in time, and possessing weight. They belong to the atomistic world. Vibrating and resembling living entities, they serve as vehicles for ideas and thoughts, carrying information and emotions. Light is essential, as prints cease to exist in a dark atmosphere.

During the process, bonds are made between those two dimensions (particle & wave model) when we put down ideas and emotion on paper, and vice versa.

the receiver/observator hold the responsability of observation and lecture. Without you, our experiments would not find any destinations points, nor confirmation of existence.

As the recipient of the Ephemeris, you benefit from a materialistic perspective. You have the opportunity to experience Chromatic's riso filters in natural sunlight, engaging your senses through smell and touch, providing the most immersive experience. This publication delves into randomness, chance, and unpredictability. While there is no real randomness in the studio, what occurs is the manifestation of energy and creation through quantum fields. Our laboratory is guided by the theory of chaos—coherent, yet with too many factors to fully comprehend its trajectory. We focus on parameters we can influence: emotions, ideas, and thoughts. We give ourselves the space to connect with our own thoughts, ideas, actions, and choices, observing the outcomes, mainly manifested in riso prints. These prints are soon dispatched to you, and we hope you enjoy a wonderful exploration. Crafted with benevolent intentions, the prints aim to elevate the world's emotional state. We trust that you will have an enjoyable time appreciating these colors and lights, and we look forward to connecting with you soon. This book is printed using risography, available in limited and numbered editions. Additionally, it exists in a virtual dimension: the internet.
